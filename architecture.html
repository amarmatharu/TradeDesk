03Position Lifecycle & Learning Loop

OPENApproved trade becomes a live position
MONITOR (60s)Hard rules: stop hit → exit · T1 hit → trim 50% + move stop to breakeven · T2 → close · 15d flat → time stop. AI checks thesis every 5 min.
CLOSEDExit publishes CRITICAL bus event → auto-triggers Journal
JOURNALPost-mortem: what worked/failed, lessons, quality score, pattern tags, R-multiple
LEARNINGSPer-pattern win-rate + avg-R → injected back into Scout & Research prompts. The system improves with every trade.
